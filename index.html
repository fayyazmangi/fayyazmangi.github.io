What is a Breathing City?

A Breathing City is one that values sustainability and supports eco-conscious urban living. It includes:

  • Urban green spaces and forests
  • Clean energy and reduced emissions
  • Eco-friendly transport systems
  • Smart recycling and waste management
  • Active citizen education and awareness

Urban Air Quality Challenges

Air pollution in Karachi is harming health, nature, and quality of life.

  • Widespread respiratory illnesses
  • Damage to biodiversity and ecosystems
  • Massive economic and healthcare burden
  • Pollutants: PM2.5, NO2, Ozone
  • Lack of sustainable planning policies

Solutions for a Breathing City

Our roadmap to a greener, healthier future includes:

  • Urban forestry and vertical gardens
  • Public EVs and hybrid transportation
  • Community waste management programs
  • Solar and wind-powered infrastructure
  • Ongoing community education campaigns
